Application

In order to sign up for any of our properties, a filled up application from everybody in your group is required. Please provide the following information online and upon approval of your application, the Lease documents will be prepared for the applicants signature.


Submission of application does not mean reservation of a property…... The application must be approved and application fee / management fee must be received by PTP Management prior to taking the property off the market and reserving a unit for your group. Please note that we do have a “First Come, First Serve” policy for leasing out the properties. Once we recieve all the applications from everyone, we will contact and set up an appointment for finalizing the lease and/or reservation of the property. If you have any questions, you can contact PTP Management at (734) 657-2096 or via e-mail: info@um-housing.com.
